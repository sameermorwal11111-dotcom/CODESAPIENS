====================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
====================================================================

ERROR: No trained classifiers found
SOLUTION: Run Option 1 or 4 to capture face data first

ERROR: Could not open webcam
SOLUTION: 
  - Close other apps using webcam
  - Restart VS Code
  - Check webcam is connected

ERROR: Face not detected during capture
SOLUTION:
  - Better lighting (avoid shadows)
  - Face should be clear and frontal
  - Keep distance 30-60cm from camera
  - Remove glasses/sunglasses if possible
  - Vary angles and expressions

ERROR: Not enough images for training
SOLUTION: Need at least 10 images, capture more
